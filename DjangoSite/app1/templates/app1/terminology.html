Stock Trading Terminology Glossary

Term Definition
Stock Symbol A unique series of letters assigned to a security for trading purposes (e.g., AAPL for Apple).
Market Capitalization The total market value of a company's outstanding shares of stock.
Dividend Yield The ratio of a company's annual dividend compared to its share price, expressed as a percentage.
Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio A valuation ratio comparing a company's current share price to its per-share earnings.
Volume The total number of shares traded during a specific period.
Moving Average (MA) A stock's average price over a set period used to smooth out price data and identify trends.
Volatility The degree of variation of a trading price series over time, indicating risk or uncertainty.
Bull Market A market condition where prices are rising or expected to rise.
Bear Market A market condition where prices are falling or expected to fall.
Support Level A price level where a stock tends to find buying interest and may stop falling.
